Last updated: [DATE]

Data We Collect

DevNotes stores your notes and categories locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. If you enable Google Drive sync, your notes are backed up to a single file in your own Google Drive account.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data remains on your device or in your own Google Drive account.

Google OAuth

DevNotes uses Google OAuth solely to authenticate you and access your Google Drive for backup purposes. We request only the drive.file scope, which limits access to files created by DevNotes only.

Third Party Services

We use Google Drive API for optional cloud backup. Google's privacy policy applies to that data: https://policies.google.com/privacy
