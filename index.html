Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08.03.2026

Data We Collect

DevNotes is built with privacy-first architecture. We store your notes and categories locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. If you enable Google Drive sync, your data is backed up exclusively to your private storage.

Data We Do Not Collect

Your data belongs to you. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All information remains isolated on your device or within your personal Google Drive account.

Google OAuth & Security

We use Google OAuth solely for authentication and secure backup. DevNotes requests only the drive.file scope, ensuring we can only access files created specifically by this application.

Third Party Services

Cloud backups are powered by the Google Drive API. Please note that Google's Privacy Policy applies to data stored within their infrastructure.
